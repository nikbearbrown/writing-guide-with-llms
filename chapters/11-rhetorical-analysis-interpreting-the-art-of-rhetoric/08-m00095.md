# Learning Outcomes {#sect-00001}

By the end of this section, you will be able to:

Evaluate the elements of a rhetorical analysis.

Identify and correct errors involving mixed sentence structures.

Evaluate an essay for clarity, coherence, and language.

At various points in your writing, especially after you complete the
first draft, check the rubric provided here. Your instructor is likely
to use a similar rubric to evaluate your rhetorical analysis. Aligning
your writing to applicable points in the rubric---especially to those
reflecting a score of 5--- will keep you focused and guide you in your
work.
