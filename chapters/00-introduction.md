## A Note about AI

This book teaches writing across genres in an era when fluent prose is cheaply produced on demand. Each chapter has its own *A note about AI* — or, in many chapters, an integrated discussion of the LLM question under a chapter-specific heading. The unifying principle: **the model cannot do the work the genre requires; it can help you think about the work you have done.** Profiles require interviews. Memoir requires memory. Literacy narratives require lived literacy. Reasoning requires reasons. The model is fluent at the texture of every genre and absent from the substance. Use the chapter notes to see where, in each genre, the boundary falls. The voice you develop is the voice the model cannot produce.
